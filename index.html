Dread is a TTRPG meant to be played with a Jenga tower to represent building tension across the course of the game. With the rise of online gaming, there is a desire for digital supplements to this game mechanic (mostly by me). So, here's my attempt at making a digital tool for playing Dread online, modeled after Pokemon HG/SS's Voltorb Flip Minigame.

  • Once the storyteller starts the timer, players have 30 seconds to choose the tile you'd like to flip.
  • Each row/column header will show a number correspond to the amount of death tiles in that group. These values only appear once the timer has been started.
  • Flipping an apple means you're safe, while flipping a skull means you're dead.
  • Once a skull has been revealed, reset the game and continue.
